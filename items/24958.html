Paraphrase

Receipt of payment: A receives from B 15 5/12 shekels of silver. This is a partial payment according to the promissory note (u’iltu) concerning 18 shekels of silver, price (šīmu) of sheep. Broken passage. Their mutual accounts are to be settled (epuš nikkassi qatû) until (adi) Tašrīt (VII) of the 30th(?) year. 2 witnesses (including Nabû-ēṭir-napšāti/Iddināya//(Ea-)ilūtu-bāni and Ana-muhhi-Nabû-amur, slave [qallu] of Zēr-Bābili//(Ea-)ilūtu-bāni) and the scribe. B is paid (eṭēru Stat.) the silver for the vats from the hands of (ina qātē) A.
 
A = Šulāya/Arad-Nabû//Ea-ilūtu-bāni; B = Lūṣi-ana-nūr-Marduk/Nabû-mukīn-zēri//Ilī-bāni; Scribe = Nabû-nādin-ahi/Nabû-šumu-ukīn//Ēdu-ēṭir
