Paraphrase

Loan of silver: B owes A 2 shekels of white scrap silver (kaspu peṣû nuhhutu). He will give the silver on 23rd of Ulūl (VI) of Darius 29th year. 3 witnesses and the scribe.
 
A = Nabû-bullissu/Lâbâši-Marduk//Nūr-Papsukkal; B = Nabû-šumu-uṣur/Mušēzib-Bēl//(Ea-)ilūtu-bāni; Scribe = Nabû-ēṭir/Nabû-ahhē-bulliṭ//Iddin-Papsukkal
