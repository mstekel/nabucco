Paraphrase

Loan of barley: B owes A 2;1 kor (396 l) of barley, without interest (qaqqadu). He will give the barley in Ayyār (II), together with a (yearly) interest (hubullu) of 0;1 kor per 1 kor of barley (20% p.a.). B will not plough (erēšu) the arable land (zēru) of another plot of land (qaqqaru). The clause about the fine in case B ploughs another land is broken. 1 witness (Šulāya//(Ea-)ilūtu-bāni) and the scribe.
 
A = Nabû-šumu-iškun/Puhhuru//(Ea-)ilūta-bāni; B = […]/Libluṭ//Ša-pî-kalbi; Scribe = Rēmūt-Gula/Mannu-kī-Nabû
