Paraphrase

Dialogue document concerning the purchase of a girl: A asks B to sell him fD, the young woman (ṣahirtu) belonging to C who is at the disposal of (ina pāni) B. A (in the text B) weighs out and pays (hāṭu-nadānu) the stipulated amount of 1 mina of silver. B (in the text A) guarantees (pūtu našû) against transgressors (sēhû) and claimants (pāqiru). 7 witnesses and the writer of the promissory note (šāṭir uˀilti).
 
A = Šulāya; B = Šūzubu; C = Ilu-bāni; fD = fŠāhitti; Scribe = Nabû-ušēzib/Balāssu-uṣur
