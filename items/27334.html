Paraphrase

Purchase of field: A purchases a half share of a field (eqlu) for 2 ½ minas of silver from B1 and his mother fB2. The plot of land comprises a date garden (kirî gišimmari) and wasteland (kišubbû) and is located in the district (erṣetu) of the Ninurta Temple inside of Uruk. Its upper side borders on the city wall (dūr āli), while its lower side, upper front and lower front border on (the properties of) C1, C2 and C3. The upper front also borders on a street (sūqu). No cardinal directions are given in the record. Its upper side measures 350 cubits (175 m), its lower side and upper front 300 cubits (150 m) and its lower front 200 cubits (100 m). The plot presumably is the first half of the land bought by A from B1 and B2 one year later (BM119872). The transaction is concluded in the presence (ina ušuzzi) of the governor (šākin ṭēmi) of Uruk (Ahhēšāya) and the bishop (šatammu) of Eanna (Balāṭu). 14 witnesses and the scribe. Fingernail impressions (ṣupru) of the sellers.
 
A = Mušēzib-Marduk/Kiribtu; B1= Bēl-ahhē-iddin/Kudurru; fB2 = fNasqat; C1 = Zākiru, aškāpu (leatherworker); C2 = Eanna-ibni, pahāru (potter); C2 = Zibāya/Ērešu; Scribe = Bēl-īpuš//Šamaš-bāri
