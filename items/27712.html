Paraphrase

Partly transfer of dowry: Along with his daughter fC, B promised to give as a dowry (nudunnû) 4 minas of silver, the (slave) D1 and 55 shekels of silver, the price (šīmu) of fD2, probably a weaver (iš-x-x-tu4, [išpartu]), to A. From these items, he herewith gives the silver amount for fD2. 5+ witnesses (including Nabû-mušētiq-uddi/Nabû-zēru-ukīn//Sîn-šadûnu) and the scribe.
 
A = Mušēzib-Bēl/Mukīn-zēri//Balīhu; B = Bēl-kāṣir/Nabû-šumu-šakin//Sîn-šadûnu; fC = fSunnā; D1 = Nabû-dīnī-īpuš; fD2 = fPān-nanāya-adaggal, išpartu(?) (weaver); Scribe = Nabû-šumu-līšir/Balassu//Šangû-Ištar-Bābili
