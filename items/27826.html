Archive Ṣāhit-ginê A

Type and content Leases

Paraphrase

Lease of arable land for joint cultivation: A rents out for cultivation (ana errēšūti nadānu) 30 square kor (405,000 m2) of arable land (zēru) to B. 15 kor of the land are located in the hūpu-land and administered by D1. The other 15 kor of land are located in the hammašatu-land and administered by D2. Both form part of the crown land (nakkandu ša šarri) under the responsbility of C, deputy (šanû) of the treasurer (mašennu), and were rented by A for cultivation (ana errēšūti ṣabātu). Lessor and lessee will plough (erēšu) the land together and share the costs for transport (gimru) and for the removing (nasāhu), to be paid to the administrators D1 and D2, as well as the costs for the farmer (ikkaru). Broken passage. 3 witnesses and the scribe. Each party has taken a copy.
 
A = Bēl-uballiṭ/Iqīša//Ṣāhit-ginê; B = Niqūdu, slave (qallu) of Bēlšunu; C = Bēl-šumu-iškun; D1= Nādinu; D2 = Kalbāya; Scribe = Šamaš-zēru-ušebši/Marduk-šumu-ibni//Dābibī
