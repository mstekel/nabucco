Paraphrase

Fixation of interest on a silver debt. Related to a house sale.
B1 and his wife (fB2) owe 1 mina and 38 ½ shekels of silver to A, on which they should pay a yearly interest of 20% on a monthly basis starting from the 1st of Simān (III) of Nabonidus 5th year onwards. The indebted silver is what remains (to be paid to A for the loan of 2 minas and 10 shekels that he lent to the couple) for the purchase of C's house. The sale is recorded in BM32871 (= Nbn 85) and the issue of A's loan to the couple recounted in BM33945 (= Nbn356). See also MMA79.7.35 and BM31675 (= Nbn 1104).
 
A = Iddin-Marduk/Iqīšāya//Nūr-Sîn; B1 = Apla-dād-natan/Addia; fB2 = fBunanītu, wife of B1; C = Ibāya/Ṣillāya//Nagāru
