Paraphrase

Contract for performance of prebendary duties: The share of B1 of the prebend of B1 and B2 before Kāribu of the shrine of Marduk consisting of a monthly instalment of 4 large (rabbû) takkasû-cakes is at the disposal (ina muhhi) of A. Each month A will give the cakes of the rations (kurrummatu) to B1. B1 in return will give one uzāru-cloth to A. 7 witnesses and the scribe (Bēl-nāṣir/Mudammiq-Nabû//Rab-banê).
 
A = Damqiya/Marduk-šumu-ibni/Isinnāya; B1 = Iddin-Nabû/Nabû-bān-zēri//Nappāhu; B2 = Šumu-iddin/Nabû-bān-zēri//Nappāhu (elder brother of B1);
